About Cyber Buzz


Cyber Buzz is the prestigious tech symposium hosted annually by Amity International School Sector 43, Gurugram. From 4 events to a dozen this event has grown to be a learning experience for tech savvy students across Delhi NCR, enhancing their creativity in the field.
It is not everyday that Cyber enthusiasts get a Call To Arms. Cyber Buzz 2021 will not be just a test of knowledge, but also of skills in an Open Battlefield. With 11 events covering the wide domain of computers and technology, Cyber Buzz XIX will truly be a test of the capability and tech quotient of the participants.

Our Events

Create a world, master another. Capture reality, tweak it later. Create Art, make it functional. From instructing computers to making the best ones, the events leave no aspect of Cyber age untouched.
